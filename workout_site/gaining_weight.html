When you say you want to gain weight, some may look at you funny. There is a false belief in the fitness industry that gaining weight is bad. Gaining weight is completely healthy, as long as you do it the right way! First thing we need to address is diet. In order to gain weight you must eat in a caloric surplus. A caloric surplus means you are eating more calories than your body burns throughout the day. Click here for a calorie intake calculator. This can be difficult to do during the day. Start by adding a little more protein and carbs to each meal. You want to focus on carbs like rice, potatoes, oatmeal, fruits, and vegetables. You also want to focus on eating protein. Some good proteins to eat are whey protein powder, chicken, beef, and fish. Next thing we need to address is your workout routine.
